History & Foundation

Green Valley High School was founded in 1995 by visionary educators and philanthropists.

It began with just 50 students and 5 teachers in a modest building.

By 2000, the school grew to 300 students and 20 teachers.

In 2005, a larger campus with modern classrooms and labs was established.

The school has consistently emphasized academic excellence and holistic education.

Every year, cultural programs and community events celebrate the school's anniversary.

The founders' vision continues to guide the school's development.

The institution is renowned for producing successful graduates in various fields.

Green Valley High School remains committed to nurturing creativity and responsibility.

The school has evolved into a leading educational establishment in the region.

Mission & Vision

Foster creativity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning.

Develop responsible citizens with strong moral values.

Vision: To be recognized as a leading institution for academic excellence and social contribution.

Empower students with knowledge, skills, and values for global success.
